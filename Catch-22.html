Product Description

Catch-22 by Joseph Heller is a satirical masterpiece about the absurdities of war. Set during World War II, it follows the story of Captain John Yossarian, a U.S. Army Air Force B-25 bombardier, as he navigates the contradictions and madness of military bureaucracy.

Features

  • A biting critique of war and bureaucracy
  • Dark humor combined with sharp social commentary
  • Regarded as one of the greatest novels of the 20th century
