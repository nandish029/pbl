Product Description

Crime and Punishment by Fyodor Dostoevsky is a gripping psychological novel exploring themes of morality, guilt, and redemption. It follows the story of Raskolnikov, a poverty-stricken student in St. Petersburg, who commits a murder and grapples with the moral consequences of his crime.

Features

  • A profound exploration of human psychology and morality
  • Rich, detailed depiction of 19th-century Russian society
  • Considered one of the greatest novels of world literature
